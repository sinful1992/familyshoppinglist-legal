Family Shopping List

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from the app by following these steps:

  1. Open Family Shopping List on your device
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Data That Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account profile (email address, display name)
  • All shopping lists you created
  • All items you added to shopping lists
  • All receipt images you uploaded
  • All urgent items you created
  • Your family group membership
  • Your spending and budget data
  • Notification tokens and preferences
  • All locally stored app data

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • All personal data is deleted immediately from our servers
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data (which cannot identify you) may be retained
  • If you had an active subscription, cancellation is handled through Google Play. Please cancel your subscription in Google Play before deleting your account

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the app or need assistance deleting your account, contact us at:

privacy@familyshoppinglist.app

Please include the email address associated with your account, and we will process your deletion request within 30 days.
